about me

hello

I’m a 25 year-old interactive and print designer based in Blacksburg, Virginia. I am fluent in the Adobe Creative Suite, HTML, CSS and the Wordpress development platform.



I’ve designed for magazines, newspapers, non profits, universities and small businesses. If you’re looking for someone who can design anything from a brochure to a fully-loaded web site with a content management system, you’re in the right place. I like working with small businesses and start ups, especially local ones, so contact me at mary@dreamboxcreates.com if you have a project that you'd like to collaborate on.



When I’m not working, I'm usually hanging out with my bagle hound, hunting for - and usually failing to find - decent east coast Mexican food, or reading just about anything.
